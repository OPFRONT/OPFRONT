about us

Our team consists of a well respected optometrist, hard working software engineers and a handful of creative folks. We are on a mission to make the life of independent optometrists much simpler with our integrated online store system.

Optometrists are currently seeing their shares on the eyewear market slowly fading away because of the e-commerce revolution. Our goal is help them grow their business and focus on their true passion: patients' eyesight.
